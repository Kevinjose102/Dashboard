Silksong

The Knight

The Knight in Hollow Knight is a silent, mysterious warrior exploring the ruined kingdom of Hallownest. Armed with a Nail, it uncovers secrets about its origins and battles to stop a spreading infection.

Hornet

Hornet is a fierce and agile warrior, often seen as both an adversary and ally to the Knight. She wields a needle and thread, and her agility and combat skills make her a formidable foe.

Hollow Knight

The Hollow Knight is a powerful Vessel created to contain the infection spread by the Radiance. Chosen by the Pale King, it was sealed away to protect Hallownest but eventually became corrupted by the infection it was meant to contain.

Grimm

Grimm is the enigmatic leader of the Grimm Troupe, a traveling circus with dark, mysterious ties. Despite his theatrical appearance, Grimm is a powerful and dangerous foe, testing the Knight’s resolve in a battle that requires skill, precision, and perseverance.

Mantis Lords

The Mantis Lords are the leaders of the Mantis Tribe, known for their honor and combat prowess. They challenge the Knight to a test of strength in a precise, fast-paced battle, rewarding the Knight's victory with safe passage through their tribe's domain.

Soul Master

The Soul Master is a powerful and arrogant foe who seeks to master the essence of soul within Hallownest. He wields potent soul magic and can manipulate his form during battle, ultimately revealing the consequences of his obsession with power.
